Pink Lemonade

Description

Pink lemonade is a wonderfully refreshing drink during the hot summer months. Garnish with mint leaves and orange or lemon slices.

Ingredients

  • 9 cups water
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup cranberry juice, chilled ice as needed

Stpes

  1. Step 1: Combine water, sugar, lemon juice and cranberry juice.
