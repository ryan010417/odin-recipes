Marinated Grilled Pork

Description

This marinated pork loin cooks up nicely on grill. It tastes best when marinated for 24 hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Stpes

  1. Step 1: Whisk Salad dressing
  2. Step 2: Preheat
  3. Step 3: Remove Chicken from marinade
  4. Step 4: Cook Chicken under preheat grill
